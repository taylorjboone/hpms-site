Transportation Performance Management (TPM) is a strategic approach that uses system information to help transportation agencies, including WVDOT, make investment and policy decisions to achieve transportation system performance goals.

TPM should be a regular, ongoing process. Federal rules for State Departments of Transportation (DOTs) and Metropolitan Planning Organizations (MPOs) related to TPM include:

  • Interagency Coordination
  • Establish Performance Targets
  • Support National Goals in the Planning Process
  • Report Progress to the United States Department Of Transportation (USDOT)
